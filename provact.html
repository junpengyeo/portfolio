The Problem

As the world comes to a standstill due to Covid-19 and travel restrictions, working from home is becoming increasingly common. As a result, there has been a rise in demand for platforms to replace face-to-face interactions. However, existing conferencing platforms such as Zoom and Google Meets do a poor job at facilitating interactions and networking in a professional manner.

Introducing Provact

A video conferencing platform for all your online interaction needs. Provact is designed with organic and professional networking in mind. Not only does it create opportunities for engagements amongst speakers and participants, Provact also empowers participants to initiate these opportunites!
